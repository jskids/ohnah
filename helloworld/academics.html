Academic Track

The ABM strand is for students interested in business, finance, and entrepreneurship. It focuses on subjects like accounting, economics, marketing, and business ethics. Students in this strand develop leadership and management skills, preparing them for careers in business ownership, financial management, and corporate leadership.

The GAS strand is for students who are still exploring their career options. It offers a mix of subjects from different strands, including business, humanities, and science, making it a versatile choice. This strand is ideal for students planning to pursue careers in teaching, business, or any general field that does not require a specialized high school track.

The HUMSS strand is for students passionate about social issues, communication, and critical thinking. It covers subjects such as philosophy, political science, psychology, and writing. This strand prepares students for careers in law, journalism, social work, education, and other fields that require strong analytical and communication skills

The STEM strand is designed for students who excel in science and mathematics. It focuses on subjects like physics, chemistry, biology, and advanced mathematics, preparing students for careers in engineering, medicine, and technology-related fields. This strand develops problem-solving and analytical skills essential for scientific research and innovation.

The ICT strand focuses on technology-related fields, such as programming, networking, and multimedia. Students learn about computer systems, coding, and digital communication, making them ready for careers in software development, cybersecurity, web design, and IT support. This strand is ideal for those who want to work in the fast-evolving tech industry
