Mission

Athens Academy is committed to develop well-rounded and productive individuals imbued with Christian values, reflected in their way of life and service to others for the Honor and Greater Glory of God.

Vision

An institution that advocates excellence through the integration of Godly principles and hard work in service to God, Country, and Humanity.
